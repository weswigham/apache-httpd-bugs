 DreamE Annoucement For June 2001 
 
 European Artists Oil On Canvas Exhibition
 
 Hawk Alfredson first solo exhibit was in 1975 at the Limner Gallery.  The Swedish born artist has become the darling of the New York underground art scene.  Since showing, Hawk has had 60 group and over 20 solo showing from New York to California.  His w orks are shown not only at prestigious galleries, but also at exotic locations such as night clubs and artists' cafes. With his northern european influence predominates Hawk's painting. The color, detail and strange scenes are reminiscent of Bosch and Br uegel.  The similarity, however, ends there.  Hawks's painting embodies the paradox of timelessness. 
 
 Dream Ambassador XVI -- Hawk Alfredson
 http://mirror.dreame.cc/artists/Hawk_Alfredson/gallery.shtml
    
 
 Spyral Intrusion -- Hawk Alfredson
 http://mirror.dreame.cc/artists/Hawk_Alfredson/gallery.shtml
    
 
 FRAME FALLING OVER SLEEPER -- Hawk Alfredson
 http://mirror.dreame.cc/artists/Hawk_Alfredson/gallery.shtml
     
 
 VENUS WITH SATIN -- Nickolai Atanassov 
 http://mirror.dreame.cc/artists/Nickolai_Atanassov/gallery.shtml
     
 
 HUMAN PASSION -- Nickolai Atanassov
 http://mirror.dreame.cc/artists/Nickolai_Atanassov/gallery.shtml
     
 
 THE BATTLE -- Nickolai Atanassov
 http://mirror.dreame.cc/artists/Nickolai_Atanassov/gallery.shtml
     
 
 Nickolai Atanassov was born in Pleven, Bulgaria, He applied his skills working several years for a design firm based in the Czech Republic.  While in college, he started learning painting inspired by the old masters" techniques. After graduating, he cont inued mastering
 his skills in painting in the private school of the world famous artist Michail Kamberov. Since 1992, Atanassov has participated in many art shows in Bulgaria. He has sold his works to galleries in Germany, Austria and private collections in Switzerland,  the Netherlands, France, and the USA. In 1998, won his firs International competition for painting. In his works, Nickolai searches for the subtle balance between the old masters and modern art. They carry the play of light displayed through the languag e of many techniques and the warmth of his heart. 
 
 European Artists Group Exhibition July 14, 2001
