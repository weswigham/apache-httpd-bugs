 <p><font face="Arial">The Lady Riders have set tryout dates and times for our U14 Y-League
 team as follows:<br>
 <br>
 Cristin Burtis, coach of the team has requested that any interested player MUST register for this tryout in advance. <br>
 <br>
 The cutoff date for registration is March 4th. <br>
 <br>
 Players may call the office at 516-735-2277 to register over the phone.  Players may also register in person at the Lady Riders booth at the LIJSL convention. Cristin will attend the convention and will be available to speak to the players directly.<br>
 <br>
 The Long Island Lady Riders, established in 1994, and entering our ninth season, continues to provide the best opportunity for Long Island female
 soccer players at all levels. <br>
 <br>
 Tryout Information:<br>
 Date            Time            Location<br>
 March 9 --- 9:00-1:00*      Polytech<br>
 March 10 -- 9:00-1:00*      Polytech<br>
 March 16        TBD         Polytech<br>
 March 17        TBD         Polytech<br>
 March 23-24**   TBD         Polytech<br>
 * Players divided into two hour blocks (9-11 and 11-1)<br>
 **Invite back weekend only</font></p>
