 *** The Elite Soccer Education Leader in Nassau is now GLOBALL SOCCER ***
 http://www.backofthenet.com
     Globall Soccer Concepts is bringing the European training experience one step closer to dedicated Long Island players through its Elite Training Courses.
 
 Globall's top level training staff will conduct specialized training similar to how European clubs like Bayern Munich, Ajax Amsterdam and A.C. Milan develop athletes into renowned soccer players.
 
 Starting in January, Elite Training Courses will be conducted at the following indoor locations: 
 
 Tuesdays = Coindre Hall in Huntington - 6:00 to 7:30 (Starting date January 8, 2002)
 Sundays = Glen Cove High School - 4:00 to 5:30 pm (Starting date January 6, 2002)
 Sundays = Globall Soccer Arena in Northport - 2:00 to 3:30 pm (Starting date January 6th)
 
 Cost per session: $20 per player
 
 To reach the next level of your soccer development please visit www.globallsoccer.com or call (516) 671-1585 to register.  Limited enrollment so at quickly to secure your spot.

 USSF SOCCER REFEREE   ENTRY LEVEL COURSE
 Presented by: Eastern NY Youth Soccer (ENYYSA)
 Hosted by: Westchester Soccer Referee Organization (WSRO)
 
 Friday  January 25, 2002 7 PM - 10:00 PM
 Saturday   January 26  9 AM - 3:30 PM
 Friday  February 1  7 PM - 10:00 PM
 Saturday   February 2  9 AM - 3:30 PM
 
 Ardsley Middle School, located on Ashford Avenue in Ardsley, east of the Saw
 Mill River Parkway and west of the Sprain Brook Parkway and Central
 Avenue-Yonkers.
 Directions: from the SMRP: exit 17 (right turn if going north; loop around if
 going south) continue on Ashford Avenue (you will pass about 7 or 8 streets);
 you will first see playground in front on your right; school is in back.
 >From Bronx River Parkway going south, exit 11 (Ardsley Road) turn right &
 continue past Central Park Avenue (Yonkers); Ardsley  Road becomes Ashford
 Avenue; after crossing the Sprain Brook Pkwy and Old Sprain Rd, the school will
 be on your left.
 Large Group Instruction (LGI) Room, located in the pool area; enter on the side
 of the building opposite the soccer field in the back of the school.
 
 Course Fee   $65.00 (includes USSF Registration $25.00 (for the year 2002
 upon passing course)
 
 
 Send a check for $65.00 payable to WSRO; complete information below and mail to
 WSRO c/o J. Fischoff  17 Rutgers Place, Scarsdale NY 10583-4905
 
 
 We will send you a reminder 8 days before the course start date.  If you need to
 cancel, a full refund will be made if we are notified within 4 days of the
 course start date. Inclement weather notice: 914 472-3196 or eysoccer.com.  The
 18 hours are prescribed by USSF and cover all phases of soccer referee
 preparation.
