 GLOBALL ANNOUNCES ITS SPRING/SUMMER CAMP SCHEDULE :: Globall is centrally located in the city of Glen Cove
 ...........................................................
  
 Peter Zaratin Creates a Stellar Soccer Camp
 
  
 
 Glen Cove, NY - January 6, 2002 - 
 The Globall Soccer Complex in Glen Cove is the premiere soccer training facility on Long Island.  This 100-acre estate, located off the Long Island Sound, is exclusively for Globall Soccer Camps. 
 
 With its private beach, running trails, numerous training fields and overnight accommodations the Globall Soccer Complex allows players to enjoy developing in a safe, captivating learning environment with Globall's professional training staff.
 
 Globall camps focus on developing the technical, tactical, physical, and psychological components of the game for teams, field players and goalkeepers.  
 Spring Camp Dates:
 
 
 April 1 - 5, 2002  (Will use Globall Soccer Bubble during inclement weather)
 
 Summer Camp Dates:
 
 July 8 - 14, 2002
 July 15 - 19, 2002
 July 22 - 26, 2002
 July 29 - August 2, 2002
 
 August 5 - 9, 2002
 August 12 - 16, 2002
 August 19 - 23, 2002
 August 26 - 30, 2002
 
 Two-week camps available
 
 For a free brochure or for additional information on the Globall Soccer Complex in Glen Cove please call (516) 671-1585 or visit www.globallsoccer.com.
 
 
 
 
 
 Company Website: http://www.globallsoccer.com
