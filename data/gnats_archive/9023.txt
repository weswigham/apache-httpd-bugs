 OUSC 35th Annual RUDY LAMONICA Indoor Tournament
 
 Are you ready to do THE RUDY?
 
 The new format for this exceptional tournament made its debut last year and the reviews on the Message Board to this sold-out tournament was a loud and clear big success.  This year teams can expect the same.  Don't get shut out of this expected sold-out  tournament.
 
 *** Do The Rudy! ***
 http://www.backofthenet.com/tournaments/ousc-2002-indoor-app.pdf
     Last year over 2000 Children participated in the Oldest Youth Indoor Soccer Tournament in The US.  This year is shaping up to be even Better.  In addition to our interactive cafeteria, 11,000 Square Foot Composite Playing Surface, Two referee system  and a minimum of four 10-minute preliminary games, followed by Semi's and Finals, with awards for the top 4 teams in each bracket.  This year we expect to build on last years Success.  In recognition of the 35th Anniversary of the Rudy Lamonica Memorial  Indoor Tournament, all coaches will receive a commemorative soccer ball.  We expect WPLJ Radio, The New York Power and Other Local Sports Teams to make appearances throughout the Tournament.  Don't be left out in the Cold, Registrations are filling up fa st.  For more information please E-Mail oceansidesoccer@aol.com or contact: Pete Bussa at 516-536-7808.
 
 To Register:  Complete and return the application (DOWNLOAD from APPLICATION PAGE on BOTN) for each team with a check or money order for $170.00 per team payable to the Oceanside United Soccer Club and mail to OUSC Lamonica Tournament, 3091 Lawson Blvd ,  Oceanside, New York  11572.
 
 Dates are on the Application which can be downloaded from the BOTN
