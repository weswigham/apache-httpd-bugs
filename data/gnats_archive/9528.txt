 Globall Soccer Complex in Italy .... 
 
 In the summer of 2002 Globall Soccer Concepts will be 
 accommodating teams who want to experience the 
 benefits of traveling, training and playing abroad at the 
 Globall Soccer Complex in Italy.
 
 Under the guidance of Peter Zaratin and Globall's top 
 international training staff, players will be exposed to 
 new cultural experiences while benefiting from 
 competitive games and innovative training.
 
 The Globall Soccer Complex in Italy is located between 
 Venice and Trieste in a small costal village off the 
 Adriatic Sea.  Participants will train at the Complex, 
 which professional players utilize during the year, while 
 performing in state of the art soccer stadiums against 
 international competition.
 
 For scheduling or additional information on the Globall 
 Soccer Complex in Italy please call (516) 671-1585 or 
 visit www.globallsoccer.com.
