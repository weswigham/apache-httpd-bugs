 *** GU-14 Y-LEAGUE TEAM BEING FORMED ***
 http://www.backofthenet.com
     Joining April Heinrichs as our guest on JKI this Sunday will be Long Island's favorite Lady Rider and Nassau Community College head women's soccer coach Cristin Burtis.  Cristin will be announcing the details and plans for the new GIRLS U-14 Y-LEAGUE  team that will be formed within the next two months.  Just Kickin' It is heard every Sunday morning from 7 - 9am on 90.3 FM
 
 The East Meadow Soccer Club will be hosting its 10th annual World Cup Indoor Tournament on 
 January 26th & 27th, 2002.
 
 The U10, U-12 & U-15 Boys as well as the U-12, U-13, U-15 & U-16 Gils will play on January 26th with all other age groups playing on January 27th.
  
 The fields are the largest on Long Island!  With 8 vs. 8 Players On the Field!!
 U-10 through U-12 will play on a 120 foot by 100' field.  U-13 thru U-16 teams will play on a 185' X 85' field. Everyone plays off the wall soccer!						
 Other highlights include:
 6 Team Divisions
 12 Player Roster
 5,  11minute Games Guarantee
 Round Robin Play								
 Best Position & MVP Awards
 
 Join us for a great tournament! You won't be disappointed!!  Application is available on the BOTN.  Click on the EMSC World Cup logo when you log on or from the tournament page.
